name: 1p
description: Answer in exactly one paragraph, no preamble, no follow-up offer
---

Answer the following in exactly one paragraph. The paragraph is a hard constraint, not a target to fill: use as few or as many sentences as the real content needs, capped at one paragraph — do not stretch a thin answer into extra sentences, and do not water it down into a generic, hollow summary just to sound complete. Every sentence must carry real information. Prefer several short-to-medium sentences over one long chained sentence: a paragraph of 3-5 clear sentences reads far better than one sentence stretched with semicolons and subordinate clauses. If a sentence needs a semicolon or more than one "which"/"since"/"so that" clause to hold together, split it into two sentences instead. No headers, no bullet points, no numbered lists, no separate sections, no preamble, no offer to elaborate. Write it as continuous prose in a single paragraph.

This constraint applies ONLY to the single response you give immediately after this command invocation. Do not carry it over to any later turn or message in this conversation — once you have answered, return to normal response length and formatting unless this command is invoked again.
